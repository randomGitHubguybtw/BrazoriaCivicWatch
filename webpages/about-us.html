We are a nonprofit (pending 501(c)3 tax status) dedicated to centralizing Brazoria County local politics! Our priority is transparency, voter education, and local political understanding.

Our Core values

Transparency

We pledge to be forever transparent. Our donors (save for unaffiliated individuals for privacy reasons) can be found on our Financial Integrity Page. We are an organization by the people and for the people, to ensure that democracy doesn't fail in the face of ignorance. We are here for You!

Accountability

We wish to be held accountable, and hold local government accountable. We are strictly non-partisan, and meant to be an opening for the public to local politics. If an official does something shady during a meeting, here is the first place it will be!

Accessibility

We make politics accessible. Have you ever felt hopeless, unknowing when your next election is? Or who to vote for? Or who even represents you? All such information, including public places, how to run for office, government sites, and more is available here!
