Frequently Asked Questions

Who are we?

We are a nonprofit (pending 501(c)3 status) organization meant to help you know exactly what is going on in Brazoria County local politics!

How do you get your funding?

We run solely off of Donations! No paywalls, no underground deals, here for you and paid by you!

How do you get your meeting summaries?

Our meeting summaries, just like our election data and officials data, is curated and checked by our very own volunteers! We value our volunteer base, who sit through meetings as they happen and review them afterwards for detailed summaries.

How do you get your election data?

Our election data, just like our summaries and officials data, is curated and checked by our very own volunteers! We value our volunteer base, who scour official sites and social media looking for election announcements and updates.

How do you get your officials data?

Our offcials data, just like our election data and summaries, is curated and checked by our very own volunteers! We value our volunteer base, who watch election results, and quickly update our site.

Are you nonpartisan?

Nonpartisanship is not only legally required for us, as a nonprofit (pending 501(c)3 status), but also its in our bylaws! We ensure we reach out to every candidate running in an election if even only one reaches out for an interview, and we do not favor one political organization or party over any other!

Do candidates know what they're going to be asked before their interview?

Nope! We do not let candidates know what will be asked until the interview itself. Whats on camera is the first time the candidate is hearing the questions. We, however, do guarantee we will not ask arbitrary seemingly silly "gotcha questions." We take our journalism seriously!

How can I get involved?

There are plenty of ways to get involved! You can volunteer, you can donate, and, if you are a political candidate, you can reach out for an interview!
